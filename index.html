New Everest Brand Identity System

A forward-thinking brand identity designed for New Everest, a distributor of indie games curated specifically for a senior demographic. The design meticulously bridges the gap between high-stakes adventure and approachable reliability by combining a "brave" visual language with a "gentle," highly legible execution. At the core of the identity is a custom-engineered geometric lettermark that seamlessly weaves together the initials "N" and "E", a negative-space mountain summit, and an upward arrow symbolizing forward momentum. Paired with the heavy, high-visibility typography of Bowlby One SC, the brand ensures excellent readability and a heroic, trusted presence across both digital platforms and physical vehicle liveries.
